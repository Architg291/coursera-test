Egg

The egg is the organic vessel containing the zygote in which an embryo develops until it can survive on its own, at which point the animal hatches. An egg results from fertilization of an egg cell.

Rice

Rice is the seed of the grass species Oryza glaberrima or Oryza sativa (Asian rice). As a cereal grain, it is the most widely consumed staple food for a large part of the world's human population, especially in Asia and Africa.

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice usually with some sugar and salt, accompanying a variety of ingredients, such as seafood. Styles of sushi and its presentation vary widely.
